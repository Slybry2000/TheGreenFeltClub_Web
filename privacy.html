Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026 • Version: 1.0

Simple Summary

We help you track pool games. We store the info you give us so the app works. You can ask us to delete your data—see Contact below.

What we collect

  • A guest ID for anonymous accounts (we use Firebase Auth to make this).
  • If you sign in with Google, we may get your name, email, and photo (Google Sign-In).
  • Your game scores, player names, and match history (saved in Firebase/Firestore).
  • Optional location or camera access if you use those features (only with your permission).

How we use it

We use your data to save your games, show stats, let you play with friends, and keep the app working. If you use Google Sign-In we store the profile info you allow. Firebase Auth helps us manage accounts.

Sharing & Ads

We store data with Google Firebase services. If we show ads, Google AdMob may use device info to show relevant ads. We do not sell your private data.

Your choices

You can ask us to delete or export your data by emailing admin@thegreenfeltclub.com. You can also choose not to sign in with Google and use an anonymous account.

Security

We use HTTPS/TLS and Firebase security rules to help protect your data. But no internet service is perfectly safe.

Contact

Questions or requests: admin@thegreenfeltclub.com.
